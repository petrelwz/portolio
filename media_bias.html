Media Bias is a web app which aims to raise people’s awareness that their opinions can be easily manipulated by biased information, as well as make readers understand the importance of checking different sources of news reports to reduce the influence of biases.

Design Rationale

In daily life, people seldom have chance to read the exact same news story from different sources. Even if they read different people’s report about one single event, they will focus more on getting new information from the additional report other than thinking about the impact of bias or one-sided information on their opinions.

This web application singled out factors of news that could affect readers’ opinions and ask their responses. With hidden information, the app provides an environment that forces people to notice their opinion change and make people aware that they are more vulnerable to biased news than they thought.
