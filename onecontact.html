Atos is an IT services company focused on digital transformation solutions for its clients. Among Atos' offerings, Atos OneContact is a mobile app that makes convenient for Atos' clients to reach service desk .

Opportunity

Currently, clients need to log in to a website to raise a support ticket. The solution is problematic in the following ways:

The aim of the project is to build a mobile app based service desk.

My Role

I am the lead designer on this project, working closely with the product managers and engineers. I have worked on sketches, prototypes, visual designs and user testing.

Process

When I joined the OneContact team, the team had built a basic version of the app. My work so far has focused on redesigning the app. First, I created a list of proposed changes along with wireframes and presented to the OneContact team. Then, after obtaining buy-in from the team, I built a prototype. After this, I conducted a pilot with 8 users to collect data and insights that helped me fine-tune the design of the app.
